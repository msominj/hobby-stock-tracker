
Title: Hobby Stock Tracker
One-Liner: A visual inventory app for hobbyists to track craft supplies by photo and quantity, with a built-in shopping and wish list.
High-Level Functional Requirements:
Users add inventory items with a photo, category (threads, fabrics, wool, filaments, etc.), and a quantity counter.
Quantities can be decremented or updated after use; all changes are persisted locally.
The main view is a scrollable grid showing item photos with stock counts clearly visible at a glance.
Users can search or filter items by name or category to quickly locate specific stock.
Any item can be flagged as needed, automatically adding it to a shopping list or wish list for the next store visit.
